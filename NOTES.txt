alt - alternative text

href - hyper reference

target = "_blank" - to open link in next tab (if don't use this by default link will be open in same tab)
<img src="pathofimage/imagename.extension"> - to locate image from another folder	ie, <img src="images/social-flickr.png">
text-decoration: none; - it will remove underline from hyperlinks

div - devide (it will divide our page in different parts)

margin: parameter	-  margin : top right bottom left;   (imagine clock to remember this)
